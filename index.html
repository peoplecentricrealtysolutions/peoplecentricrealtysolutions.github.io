A Sanctuary of Care & Comfort

Dedicated to holistic wellbeing

At Family Style Living LLC, we believe that quality care goes beyond medical necessities; it is about creating an environment where dignity and comfort flourish. Established in Jacksonville, our facility bridges the gap between clinical nursing excellence and the warmth of a family home. We specialize in providing comprehensive nursing care tailored to individual needs, ensuring safety and peace of mind for families.

What sets us apart is our integrated approach to wellness, incorporating professional therapeutic massage to alleviate pain, improve circulation, and enhance the overall quality of life for our residents. Our team consists of licensed practitioners who are not just caregivers but companions in your loved one's journey. We are committed to fostering a supportive community where every resident feels valued, heard, and deeply cared for.

Our Core Services

Comprehensive care solutions tailored to individual needs

Skilled Nursing Care

We provide round-the-clock professional nursing supervision, medication management, and health monitoring. Our licensed staff ensures that all medical protocols are followed strictly while maintaining a gentle and compassionate bedside manner for every resident.

Therapeutic Massage

Our specialized massage therapy focuses on geriatric needs, offering pain relief, reducing inflammation, and promoting relaxation. Regular sessions are designed to improve mobility, decrease stress levels, and contribute significantly to emotional and physical well-being.

Assisted Living Support

We assist with daily living activities such as bathing, dressing, and dining, empowering residents to maintain as much independence as possible. Our goal is to provide a supportive environment that feels like home, fostering social connection and personal dignity.

The Family Style Difference

Why families in Jacksonville trust us with their loved ones

Holistic Health Integration

We don't just treat symptoms; we care for the whole person. By combining clinical nursing with therapeutic touch and massage, we address both physical ailments and emotional health, leading to better overall outcomes.
